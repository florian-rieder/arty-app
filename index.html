Arty

Boost your productivity with this all-in-one image viewer

Download now

Objective

Arty is meant as an organisational tool when working with a corpus of artworks. It aims to facilitate recurring tasks when working with images in an academic setting.

Features

Arty is an image viewer and corpus management tool for art history. Main features include organizing your images, tagging, viewing and comparing, and export to Powerpoint presentation, among other options.

Organize your images with ease

Keep all of your images organized in a folder on your computer. Each folder is a corpus of images that you can open with Arty. Annotate your images with information such as artist, title, datation, etc.

Compare

Compare up to 4 images at a time.

Export corpus to Powerpoint

Export your entire corpus or a selection of images to a Powerpint presentation, which whill be automatically captioned with the images' annotations.

For more advanced uses, a corpus can also be exported to CSV, and the application's collection file (.arty extension) is itself a JSON document
